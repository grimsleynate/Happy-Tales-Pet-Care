About Us

Mission Statement

Our mission is to give pets and their pet parents the best care they can possibly have. We are here to give you the peace of mind knowing your fur babies are being taken care of with the utmost skill and attention. As avid pet parents and busy professionals, we get it. We know how stressful it is worrying about the well-being of your fur babies all day. Here at Happy Tales we understand how much your pets mean to you, and we do our very best to alleviate any anxiety that you or your pets may have while you are away by maintaining your pets routine as best as possible in their own home.

Our Story

Before making this her full-time job, owner and operator Ashley Ferrell has spent years taking care of pets, both her own and other's. After realizing she had a passion for animal care, she decided to make a career out of it!

Happy Tales Pet Care was founded in 2018, and since then we have greatly expanded our Happy Tales Pack. We love taking on new clients and meeting new fur families! If you are interested in seeing why our Pack has gotten so big, schedule a Meet & Greet today and find out!

Service Area

Service Areas are West Branch, Rose City and Saint Helen as long as it is 20 miles from our location. Any services that are out of range will be an additional .50 cents per mile added on to the service. If you aren't sure if you are in our service range feel free to fill out the form and we will let you know!
