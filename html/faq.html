-Payment is due at time of service or upon completion of service.
-A full refund is availability if canceled before 72 hours of the service.
-If you cancel after the 72 hours, but before the 24 hours you will receive a 50% refund for the first 4 cancelled calendar days from the cancellation request date. A 100% refund I available for any additional days.
-Cancellations for visits within 24 hours will be charged in full. Cancellations for holiday visits within 72 hours will be charged in full.
-Reservations are made to plan sitter availability to clients. Therefore, clients returning home early will be required to pay for the reserved amount of time scheduled including travel time, if applicable.
