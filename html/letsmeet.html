What To Expect At The Meet & Greet

The Meet & Greet will be with Ashley, you and your fur family. Usually the meeting will last for about 30 minutes. We like to get some one-on-one time with your pets in order to make sure they are comfortable with us. Next, we will go through your pet's daily routine; where their food is, their water, leashes and treats. If your pet goes outside, we like to walk around the yard and go over their outside rules and what they like to do outside.

If you are able to we would like you to have these forms filled out and signed before the Meet & Greet, if not we will bring an extra set to fill out there. If you are unable to have the forms filled out prior to the Meet & Greet, the meeting might run upwards of an hour.
